Pixel Platformer

Created 12 themed level assets for each learning module. Limited to 16-32bit.

PlayZenik is an online STEM learning portal for children. It has a learn, build, and play idea. It combines a learning curriculum along with STEM kits and games to provide kids with the best possible learning experience. I worked together with the developers to create assets ranging from tilesets, backgrounds, enemies, and collectables based off of the 12 different themes we have. For example: electricity, space, chemical reactions. Each of the themes has it's own background, props, enemy animations, projectile animations (if any), and collectable animations. Ongoing project.

Web Assets for PlayZenik

Responsible for illustrating on-brand images to replace stock photos on landing pages.

PlayZenik is an online STEM learning portal for children. It already has a branding of playfulness with a vibrant color palette of blue and orange. I decide to use this existing branding to illustrate some simple and cartoony thumbnails and other imformative assets for the entire page.

Social Media Posts

In charge of social media presence (FB, IG, Twitter, LinkedIn).

I was in charge of TechZenik's social media post and was expected to post 2-3 times a week in addition to my regular projects. Together with the marketing team, we translate STEM content into appealling posts while staying on the theme of being playful and colorful to attract children and parents. In additional to informational posts, there were also promotional posts. All social media posts were done on Canva.

Promotional Motion Graphics

Created 3 seperate but similar themed motion graphics for new products.

Given an one week deadline to plan and create 3 promotional videos dedicated to TechZenik's new service of STEM Kits and online learning material. All done on Adobe After Effects and drawn on Paint Tool Sai.

I decided to go with a cartoony and hand drawn elements with muted colors with blue and orange as the main colors since it was the company's brand identity. While planning, I had in mind that each video will be under 1 minute and keep it short and simple since I will be targetting children. There will also be crayon styled drawn "imperfection" assets along with text to appear youthful and fun. The thumbnails are drawn seperately rather than having a screengrab since I wanted it to stand out in order to gain checks (ex have questions people would ask).

Personal Logo & Branding

Created my own personal branding with my Initials, C.C.
